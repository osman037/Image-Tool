Common Questions

Find quick answers to frequently asked questions

What is the maximum file size limit for image uploads?

You can upload images up to 20MB each.

Are my images and documents safe when resized using Quickresizer.com?

Absolutely! We process everything locally in your browser no uploads, no storage, complete private, secure and safe.

What upload formats are supported?

We support all major formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG, and WEBP. You can easily convert between any of these formats.

Can I resize multiple images at once?

Yes! You can upload a maximum of 10 images at once, resize them all with a single click, and download them as a convenient ZIP file.

Will I lose image quality after resizing?

We have developed our own image resizing algorithms to maintain quality, but it also depends on the quality of the original uploaded image. For the best image quality, always save images in PNG format.
